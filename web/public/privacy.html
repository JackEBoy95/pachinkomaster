Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

PachinkoMaster is a free browser-based app. We keep things simple: no accounts, no sign-ups, and we don't collect any personal information ourselves.

What data we store

PachinkoMaster saves your prizes, players, and board settings in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device — it is stored locally so your configuration persists between sessions. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

Advertising

PachinkoMaster displays ads served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show you personalised ads based on your browsing activity. We do not control or have access to this data.

For more information on how Google uses your data, please visit Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalised advertising at adssettings.google.com.

Cookies

We do not set any first-party cookies ourselves. Third-party cookies may be set by Google AdSense for advertising purposes as described above.

Third-party services

PachinkoMaster loads flag images from HatScripts/circle-flags (via CDN) and fonts from Google Fonts. These services may log standard web request data such as your IP address and browser type in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Children's privacy

PachinkoMaster is a general-purpose tool suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at blipstermagic@gmail.com.
