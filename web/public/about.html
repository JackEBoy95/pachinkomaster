About PachinkoMaster

PachinkoMaster is a free, physics-based random decision maker that runs entirely in your browser. Inspired by the classic Japanese pachinko machine, it lets you drop balls through a grid of pegs and watch them bounce their way to a random outcome — no algorithms, no bias, just satisfying physics.

Whether you need to settle a friendly debate, pick who buys the next round, assign chores, choose tonight's dinner, or run a full knockout tournament, PachinkoMaster makes the decision visually exciting and genuinely random.

Features

  • 🎯Custom prizes with labels, points and images
  • 👤Multi-player mode with colour-coded balls
  • 🏆Tournament mode with elimination rounds
  • 🎨Custom ball skins — flags, emoji and more
  • 🎮Preset game configs for quick starts
  • 📱Works on desktop and mobile
  • ⚡Adjustable ball size, peg density and bounciness
  • 🔗Share board configs via URL

Everything is saved locally in your browser — no account needed, no sign-up, completely free.
